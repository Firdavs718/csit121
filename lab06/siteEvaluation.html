For my website evaluation I decided to choose Towson University.

  • This websites design is unique the reason I say that's is because it got mix of Hierarchical and Random Organization.

  • The website is also very efficient, everything in the website is simple and easy to understand, user firendly.
  • The website is very engaging it is filled with photos and useful information that will keep a user in a comfortable and engaging state.
  • My only recommendation would be to make the image in the home page smaller, in my opinion it is taking up a lot of space, other than that the website looks solid.
